Request Data Deletion

You may request that your personal data associated with the Lightspeed Impact application be deleted at any time.

How to Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of your data, please send an email to:

kollandergames@gmail.com

To help us identify your account and process your request securely, please include the following information:

  • Player ID – This can be found in the game’s settings menu.

Example subject line: Data Deletion Request

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon verification, we will delete all user-provided data associated with your Player ID, such as your chosen alias, and stored gameplay records.

Confirmation and Timeline

After receiving your request, we will process the deletion as soon as reasonably possible, typically within 30 days, and confirm completion via email.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding your data or this process, please contact us at kollandergames@gmail.com.
